What are Hacker Hours?

Hacker Hours is a completely open space for people to receive programming help, work on homework or projects, or to just hang out and talk with other like-minded people. We're a community of programmers, developers, and designers, focused on helping you learn to code and get better and what you do.

This sounds awesome! How do I get involved?

We're always looking for people to become mentors and speakers for our lightning talks! If you have expertise in any programming languages and enjoy helping others learn, or have any awesome projects or technologies you would love to speak about, please get in touch with us here.
